Coffee

To us here at Human Beans, coffee is much more than drink. Coffee is a way of life. It is a great way to express yourself, you can tell a lot about a person by what they order. We believe the right cup of coffee should be match with the right person each day. That is why we take pride in sourcing our beans from trusted farms. We have built strong relationships with these farms to ensure that the coffee that you are about to drink was produced ethically from seed to cup. This allows us to confidently serve you the best drink that we can so that you can live your best day, whether that is drinking a caramel macchiato and listening to Lo-fi Beats or a simple drip coffee to go. We are proud to be humans that stand behind the beans that go in to each one of our drinks made every day.
